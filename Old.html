Browse Through the different genres of UK Rap below!

UK Trap. The UK Trap scene is the home of some of the UK's biggest artist. Artist Include D-Block Europe, Satan Dave and M-Huncho.

UK AfroSwing. This sub genre of UK rap which was developed in the 2010s and was inspired from the the genres dancehall, afrobeats and hip hop. Notable artist of this sub genre are Acen, Not3s and NSG.
UK Drill. UK Drill was inspired from the Chicago Drill scene and originated in the Brixton distrcit in South London. Notable UK Drill acts are 67, Harlem Spartans and CB (7th).
UK Garage. This genre is an electronic dance genre which orignated in the 90s. Garage tracks usually include chopped up vocals. Notable Garage acts are So Solid Crew, Flava D and Wiley.
UK Christian Rap. UK Christian Rap is not bigger enough to have sub genres inside. Christian Rap is rapping across all type of UK rap beats. The artist rapping however is usually rapping about a christian topic.
